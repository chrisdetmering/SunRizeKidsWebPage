What is SunRize Kids?

  • SunRize Kids is a fitness program designed to keep kids active, inspired, and focused
  • For 3 weeks, kids will get daily and weekly challenges which they get points for completing
  • Leaderboards allow kids to compete against their friends and other kids across the nation
  • The goal is to help our kids build healthy habits and practices in fitness and nutrition

How does it work?

  1. Each child creates an account with WodifyRise (with some help from a parent)
  2. For 3 weeks(starting on April 12) they will get daily tasks to complete, which the get points for in the App.

    3. Examples include:

    1. Play outside for 30 minutes
    2. Drink 20oz of water
    3. Eat 2 servings of vegetables
    4. Attend a live Crossfit Kids Zoom class
    5. Complete your schoolwork
    6. Take a hike as a family
  3. Weekly PDF checklists are also provided to track the completion of goals offline
  4. Live CrossFit Kids Zoom classes will be hosted twice a week
  5. Kid Friendly off-line workouts will be posted daily
